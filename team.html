Russell

My name is Russell Sims and I recently graduated from Portland State University with a B.S. in Criminology. With that I plan to start a career in Federal Law Enforcement one day. I have a passion for physical activity, from lifting pianos and pool tables, to scuba diving off the coast, and running marathons. I have lived in Portland all my life and love its access to the mountain, the coast and desert! When I’m not moving, I’m camping, hiking or reading a good book. Our company started when my roommate and I noticed a shortage of dedicated, careful and reasonably priced moving services. With our previous backgrounds in the moving business, we decided to put our skill sets and interests into action and start Portland Mobile Movers. Since then we have loved serving the Portland community with a low price, quality service. Hope we get the pleasure of moving you one day!
